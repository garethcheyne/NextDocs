# Getting Started with Dynamics 365 Business Central

Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is a comprehensive business management solution for small and medium-sized organizations. It helps you connect your financials, sales, service, and operations to streamline business processes, improve customer interactions, and make better decisions.

## What is Business Central?

Business Central is a cloud-based Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system that provides:

- **Financial Management** - Complete accounting and financial reporting
- **Sales & Marketing** - Customer relationship and sales process management
- **Service Management** - Service order and contract management
- **Operations** - Inventory, purchasing, and warehouse management
- **Project Management** - Project planning and resource allocation
- **Manufacturing** - Production planning and shop floor control

## Key Benefits

### All-in-One Solution

Business Central brings together all your business processes in one integrated system, eliminating data silos and improving efficiency.

### Cloud-Based Platform

Access your business data from anywhere, on any device, with automatic updates and built-in security.

### Microsoft Ecosystem Integration

Seamlessly integrate with Office 365, Power BI, Power Apps, and other Microsoft tools you already use.

### Scalable & Flexible

Start with the features you need and add more as your business grows. Customize with extensions and apps.

## System Requirements

### Cloud Deployment

- Modern web browser (Edge, Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
- Internet connection
- Microsoft 365 account (recommended)

### On-Premises Deployment

- Windows Server 2016 or later
- SQL Server 2016 or later
- .NET Framework 4.8 or later
- Minimum 8GB RAM
