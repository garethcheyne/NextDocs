# Fleet Operations Management

Optimize your transport operations with comprehensive fleet management capabilities including vehicle tracking, maintenance scheduling, and driver management.

## Vehicle Management

### Vehicle Registration & Documentation

#### Vehicle Database

- **VIN Tracking** - Unique vehicle identification
- **Registration Details** - License plates and renewal dates
- **Insurance Policies** - Coverage tracking and renewals
- **Ownership Records** - Lease vs. purchase documentation
- **Specification Data** - Make, model, year, capacity

#### Compliance Management

- **DOT Inspections** - Department of Transportation requirements
- **Safety Certifications** - Annual safety inspection tracking
- **Emission Testing** - Environmental compliance monitoring
- **Commercial Licenses** - CDL and operating authority
- **International Documentation** - Cross-border compliance

### Maintenance Operations

#### Preventive Maintenance

```
Maintenance Schedule:
- Oil Change: Every 5,000 miles
- Tire Rotation: Every 10,000 miles
- Brake Inspection: Every 15,000 miles
- Transmission Service: Every 30,000 miles
- Engine Overhaul: Every 150,000 miles
```

#### Maintenance Tracking

- **Service History** - Complete maintenance records
- **Vendor Management** - Approved service provider network
- **Cost Analysis** - Maintenance cost per mile tracking
- **Warranty Tracking** - Coverage period and claim management
- **Parts Inventory** - Common spare parts management

#### Breakdown Management

1. **Emergency Response** - 24/7 roadside assistance
2. **Diagnostic Assessment** - Remote and on-site evaluation
3. **Repair Authorization** - Cost approval workflows
4. **Alternative Transportation** - Backup vehicle deployment
5. **Cost Recovery** - Insurance and warranty claims

#### Qualification Requirements

- **Commercial Driver's License** - CDL class verification

- **Drug & Alcohol Testing** - DOT-required screening
- **Background Check** - Criminal history verification
- **Medical Certification** - DOT physical examination

#### Training Programs

- **Safety Training** - Defensive driving and hazard recognition
- **Vehicle Operation** - Specific equipment training
- **Regulatory Compliance** - Hours of Service rules
- **Customer Service** - Professional interaction standards
- **Technology Training** - Mobile app and GPS system usage

### Performance Monitoring

#### Key Performance Indicators

- **Safety Score** - Accident-free driving record
- **Fuel Efficiency** - Miles per gallon performance
- **On-Time Delivery** - Schedule adherence rate
- **Customer Ratings** - Service quality feedback
- **Hours of Service Compliance** - Regulatory adherence

#### Driver Scorecards

```
Performance Metrics:
- Safety Score: 0-100 points
- Fuel Efficiency: MPG vs. fleet average
- Delivery Performance: On-time percentage
- Customer Satisfaction: 1-5 star rating
- Compliance Rating: Violation count
```

### Hours of Service Management

#### HOS Regulations

- **Daily Driving Limit** - 11 hours maximum
- **Daily Duty Limit** - 14 hours maximum
- **Weekly Limits** - 60/70 hours (7/8 day cycles)
- **Required Rest** - 10 consecutive hours off-duty
- **Break Requirements** - 30-minute breaks after 8 hours

#### Electronic Logging

- **ELD Compliance** - Automatic hours recording
- **Violation Alerts** - Real-time compliance monitoring
- **Inspection Reports** - Roadside inspection preparation
- **Data Transfer** - DOT inspection data sharing
- **Record Retention** - 6-month data storage requirement
