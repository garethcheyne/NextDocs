The Stock Status function has been updated with enhanced capabilities and new interface features supporting both Business Central and Retail stock queries.

## Key Updates

### Dual System Support

Now supports both Business Central and Retail stock queries, providing comprehensive visibility across all inventory systems.

### Enhanced Functionality

Improved stock status checking across multiple systems with real-time data synchronization.

### New Stock Status Button

Added a dedicated Stock Status button to the product grid on both Opportunity and Quote forms for quick access.

## Benefits

### Comprehensive Stock Visibility

- View stock levels from multiple systems in one place
- Real-time inventory data across Business Central and Retail systems
- Consolidated view eliminates need to check multiple systems

### Improved User Experience

- Quick access to stock information directly from product grids
- Streamlined workflow without leaving current form
- Intuitive button placement for easy discovery

### Better Decision Making

- Real-time stock data to support sales processes
- Accurate availability information for customer commitments
- Reduced risk of overselling or stockouts

## Action Required

1. **Familiarize yourself** with the new Stock Status button location on Opportunity and Quote product grids
2. **Test the enhanced functionality** with both Business Central and Retail stock queries
3. **Verify data accuracy** across both systems during initial usage
